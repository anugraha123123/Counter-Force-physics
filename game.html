Balance the Forces Challenge

How to Play This Physics Game:

  1. The game starts automatically! A random Action Force will push the ball (representing an object in motion).
  2. Use the arrow keys (Left/Right) to adjust the Counter Force (reaction force) in real-time. Left decreases, Right increases.
  3. Goal: Keep the ball centered on the platform by balancing forces (Net Force ≈ 0). The ball moves based on net force!
  4. Earn points for every second you keep it balanced. If the ball falls off, game over.
  5. Levels increase: Action force changes every 10 seconds for added challenge.
  6. Learn: This simulates Newton's Third Law – counter forces keep things stable!
