What is Counter Force?

In physics, a counter force, often referred to as a reaction force, is the force that opposes an action force according to Newton's Third Law. This law states that for every action, there is an equal and opposite reaction.

The counter force is essential in understanding motion, equilibrium, and interactions between objects. It ensures that forces are balanced in pairs, even if they act on different bodies.
